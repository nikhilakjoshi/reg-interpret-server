COMPLIANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

Data Protection Requirements:
1. All personal data must be encrypted when stored
2. Access to personal data requires proper authorization
3. Data retention period is limited to 5 years maximum
4. Any data breach must be reported within 24 hours

Financial Reporting Standards:
1. All transactions must be recorded within 48 hours
2. Monthly financial reports are mandatory
3. External audit is required annually
4. All expenses over $10,000 require approval

Document Management:
1. All documents must have proper version control
2. Document approval requires at least 2 signatures
3. Changes to critical documents require board approval
4. Document retention policy: 7 years minimum
